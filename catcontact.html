How to contact us!

Contacting the Owner!

If you are looking to contact the owner of this webpage, scream "Cats are Awesome!" into the wind, and i'll know.

Contacting a cat!

If you want to find a cat, go to your local animal shelter! Animal shelters tend to have cats you can go and look at, even if you aren't planning on adopting at this moment!

If you find a stray cat without a collar you want to contact, we suggest not initiating that contact. Feral cats tend to not like humans, and may attack you or have diseases.

If you find a stray cat, We suggest you contact your local animal shelter!
